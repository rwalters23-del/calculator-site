📚 Guide: How to Choose AI Coding Tools

What are AI coding tools?

AI coding tools are assistants that help you write code faster. They use AI to autocomplete, generate code, explain concepts, and debug issues.

How to use this calculator

  • Select the AI tools you plan to use (can choose multiple)
  • Enter your weekly usage hours
  • Enter project duration in months
  • Click Calculate to see total costs

Tips for saving money

  • Start with free tiers to test each tool
  • Use Cursor's free tier for small projects
  • v0's free tier has 100 generations/month - enough for prototypes
  • Consider annual plans for 20% discounts

Q: Which AI coding tool is best for beginners?

A: Cursor or Windsurf are great for beginners. Both offer generous free tiers and are designed for full-stack development.

Q: Can I use multiple AI tools at once?

A: Yes! Many developers use Cursor for coding and ChatGPT for debugging. Just add up the costs in the calculator.

Q: Is there a free option?

A: Yes - Cursor Free (2K GPT-4o + 500 Claude messages/month), Copilot Free, and v0 Free (100 generations).

📚 What is Vibe Coding?

Vibe coding = using AI to build software while you focus on the "vibe" — design, direction, and user experience. You describe what you want, AI writes the code.

Why it saves money

  • Reduce development time by 50-80%
  • No need to hire expensive developers
  • Faster iteration cycles
  • Free to start, pay only for tools

Typical time savings by project type

  • Landing page: 80% faster (8hrs → 1.5hrs)
  • Web app: 70% faster (100hrs → 30hrs)
  • Mobile app: 60% faster (200hrs → 80hrs)
  • SaaS product: 50% faster (500hrs → 250hrs)

Q: Do I need coding experience?

A: Basic knowledge helps, but vibe coding is designed for non-experts too. You describe what you want in plain English.

Q: What can I build?

A: Web apps, mobile apps, APIs, dashboards, and more. Complex apps may still need developer review.

📚 Guide to GLP-1 Medications

GLP-1 medications are FDA-approved drugs that help with weight loss by suppressing appetite and improving blood sugar control.

Tips for saving

  • Check if insurance covers your medication
  • Consider compounded versions (lower cost)
  • Look for manufacturer discounts
  • Use prescription savings cards

Q: Are GLP-1 medications safe?

A: Yes, when prescribed by a doctor. Common side effects include nausea, diarrhea, and constipation.

Q: How long do I need to take them?

A: Most people take GLP-1 medications for 6-12 months to achieve their weight loss goals.

📚 Understanding Breakeven

Your breakeven point is when total revenue equals total costs. Beyond that point, you start making profit.

  • Fixed costs - Rent, salaries, subscriptions
  • Variable costs - Materials, shipping per unit
  • Contribution margin - Price minus variable cost

📚 Is Solar Worth It?

Solar can save you thousands over 20-30 years, especially in sunny areas with high electricity prices.

  • Average payback period - 6-12 years
  • System lifespan - 25-30 years
  • Tax credits - 30% federal tax credit through 2032
  • ROI - Typically 10-20% return on investment

Q: How long do solar panels last?

A: Most panels last 25-30 years, with gradual efficiency decline of about 0.5% per year.
