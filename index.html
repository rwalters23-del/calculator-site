📚 What is Vibe Coding?

Vibe coding is a new approach to building software where you use AI assistants to write code while you focus on the "vibe" — the overall direction, design, and user experience. You describe what you want, and AI generates the code.

Why it saves money

  • Reduce development time by 50-80%
  • Fewer developers needed
  • Faster iteration cycles

A: Basic knowledge helps, but vibe coding is designed for non-experts too. You describe what you want in plain English.

Q: What can I build?

A: Web apps, mobile apps, APIs, dashboards, and more. Complex apps may still need developer review.

📚 Guide to GLP-1 Medications

GLP-1 medications are FDA-approved drugs that help with weight loss by suppressing appetite and improving blood sugar control.

Tips for saving

  • Check if insurance covers your medication
  • Consider compounded versions (lower cost)
  • Look for manufacturer discounts
  • Use prescription savings cards

Q: Are GLP-1 medications safe?

A: Yes, when prescribed by a doctor. Common side effects include nausea, diarrhea, and constipation.

Q: How long do I need to take them?

A: Most people take GLP-1 medications for 6-12 months to achieve their weight loss goals.

📚 Understanding Breakeven

Your breakeven point is when total revenue equals total costs. Beyond that point, you start making profit.

  • Fixed costs - Rent, salaries, subscriptions
  • Variable costs - Materials, shipping per unit
  • Contribution margin - Price minus variable cost

📚 Is Solar Worth It?

Solar can save you thousands over 20-30 years, especially in sunny areas with high electricity prices.

  • Average payback period - 6-12 years
  • System lifespan - 25-30 years
  • Tax credits - 30% federal tax credit through 2032
  • ROI - Typically 10-20% return on investment

Q: How long do solar panels last?

A: Most panels last 25-30 years, with gradual efficiency decline of about 0.5% per year.
