What is TDEE?

TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) is the total number of calories you burn in a day. It includes your BMR plus all physical activity.

How to Use This Calculator

Enter your details and activity level to get your daily calorie needs. Use the goal dropdown to see how many calories you need to lose, maintain, or gain weight.

Activity Multipliers

  • Sedentary: BMR × 1.2
  • Lightly active: BMR × 1.375
  • Moderately active: BMR × 1.55
  • Very active: BMR × 1.725
  • Extra active: BMR × 1.9
