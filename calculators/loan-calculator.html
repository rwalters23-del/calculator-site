FAQ

How is loan interest calculated?

Loan interest is calculated using the amortization formula. Most loans use simple interest calculated on the remaining principal balance each month.

What's the difference between fixed and variable rate loans?

A fixed rate loan has the same interest rate for the entire term. A variable rate loan can change over time based on market conditions.

Should I pay extra on my loan?

Paying extra can save you money on interest and help you pay off the loan faster. Check if your loan has prepayment penalties first.
